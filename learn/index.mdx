---
title: "Start here: the big picture"
description: "Learn what Didit ID is for before you learn the technical pieces."
---

Didit ID helps you prove facts about people or things online without passing paper around, emailing scans, or making phone calls to check records. A company can give someone a signed digital credential, which is a proof with a checkable digital seal. The person can keep it in a wallet app, which is an app that stores credentials, and another company can check only the facts it needs. The goal is simple: make online proof feel closer to showing a trusted ID card at a desk, but faster, safer, and easier to reuse.

<Note>
  You do not need to know cryptography, wallets, issuers, or open identity standards before you read these pages. Each page defines the idea before it uses the technical word.
</Note>
